The day began late from Shannon’s house. We had waffles in the morning. Steve was as sick as ever, so we had to travel slowly. Shannon rode with us out of the city, and put us on route about 20 miles outside Richmond.

Once we hit the Colonial Parkway, we picked up speed. It was strange to think that we wouldn’t have to ride the next day. The sun was setting as we arrived at the Victory Monument in Yorktown. We ended up riding 87 miles.

We rode in the dark to a motel and ordered a pizza. Shannon will drive us to the airport, and we will arrive at PDX at 2:00 AM on Sunday morning.
